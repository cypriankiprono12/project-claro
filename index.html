Rules of the Game

  1. Each team has 5 players on the court.
  2. The game is played in four quarters.
  3. Dribbling is required to move with the ball.
  4. No physical contact is allowed; fouls are penalized.
  5. Scoring: 2 points inside the arc, 3 points outside.
